The Burns were a Russian indie rock and britpop band from Moscow active in the late 2000s.


Although not getting great recognition during their time of activity, “Waste My Time” became a staple in the “Lostwave” community, a collective who searches for the sources of unrecognized music.


“Waste My Time” was soon identified as “Avail”, now their most popular song.
